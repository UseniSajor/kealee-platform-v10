## 🎯 New Section: Specialized Capabilities

### Location
**Page:** `/development/services`
**Position:** Between "Detailed Tier Breakdown" and "Engagement CTA"

### Content Added (6 Capability Cards)

**1. C-PACE Financing**
- Commercial Property Assessed Clean Energy financing coordination
- Energy efficiency and renewable energy improvements
- C-PACE application and lender coordination
- Energy audit and scope optimization
- Project documentation and compliance

**2. Historic Tax Credits**
- Federal and state historic tax credit coordination
- Qualified rehabilitations and adaptive reuse projects
- Part 1, 2, 3 application coordination
- Secretary of Interior Standards compliance
- State historic preservation office liaison

**3. Affordable Housing Tax Credits (LIHTC)**
- Low-Income Housing Tax Credit program coordination
- Qualified affordable and workforce housing developments
- LIHTC application and allocation coordination
- Compliance monitoring and documentation
- State housing agency coordination

**4. New Markets & Opportunity Zones**
- New Markets Tax Credits (NMTC) coordination
- Qualified Opportunity Zone investment structuring
- NMTC application and CDE coordination
- Opportunity Zone structuring and compliance
- CDFI and impact investment coordination

**5. State & Local Incentives**
- TIF district and tax increment financing
- Property tax abatements and exemptions
- Economic development grants and subsidies
- Local incentive program applications

**6. Energy & Green Building Incentives**
- LEED and green building certification coordination
- Energy Star programs
- Utility rebate and incentive applications
- Federal energy efficiency tax credits
