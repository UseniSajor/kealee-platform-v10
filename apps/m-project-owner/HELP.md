## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

1. **Complete Onboarding**: When you first log in, you'll be guided through an onboarding tour that covers the basics of using Kealee.

2. **Create Your Profile**: Ensure your profile information is complete, including:
   - Name and contact information
   - Payment method (for escrow deposits)
   - Notification preferences

3. **Explore the Dashboard**: Familiarize yourself with the main dashboard where you'll see:
   - Active projects
   - Upcoming milestones
   - Pending approvals
   - Recent activity

### Navigation

- **Projects**: View all your projects
- **Contracts**: Manage all contracts across projects
- **Milestones**: Track milestone progress
- **Payments**: View payment history and escrow status

## Creating Projects

### Step 1: Basic Information

1. Click **"New Project"** from the dashboard
2. Enter project details:
   - **Name**: Descriptive name for your project
   - **Description**: Brief overview of the project
   - **Category**: Select from Kitchen, Bathroom, Addition, New Construction, Renovation, or Other
   - **Property**: Link to an existing property or create a new one

### Step 2: Project Settings

- Set your preferred timeline
- Define budget range (optional)
- Add any special requirements or notes

### Step 3: Save and Continue

Once created, your project will be in **DRAFT** status. You can:
- Edit project details
- Add readiness checklist items
- Start creating contracts

## Readiness Checklist

### Purpose

The readiness checklist ensures you have everything in place before starting construction. This helps prevent delays and cost overruns.

### Common Checklist Items

- **Finalize scope of work**: Clearly define what will be done
- **Set firm budget**: Establish your total project budget
- **Review timeline**: Understand the expected duration
- **Secure financing**: Ensure funds are available
- **Understand permit requirements**: Know what permits are needed
- **Review insurance coverage**: Verify adequate coverage
- **Plan for displacement**: If needed, arrange temporary housing

### Completing Items

1. Click on a checklist item
2. Provide the required information or upload documents
3. Mark as complete when finished
4. All **required** items must be completed before proceeding

### Status Requirements

- **DRAFT**: Can edit freely
- **READINESS**: Must complete all required checklist items
- **CONTRACTING**: Can create contracts
- **ACTIVE**: Project is underway

## Contracts

### Creating a Contract

1. Navigate to your project
2. Click **"Create Contract"**
3. Select or add a contractor
4. Define contract terms:
   - **Total Amount**: Contract value
   - **Timeline**: Expected completion date
   - **Milestones**: Break down work into milestones
   - **Payment Schedule**: When payments will be released

### Contract Features

- **Digital Signing**: Contracts are signed electronically via DocuSign
- **Automatic Escrow**: Escrow account is created automatically
- **Milestone Tracking**: Link milestones to payment releases
- **Document Storage**: All contract documents are stored securely

### Signing Process

1. Contract is created and sent to both parties
2. You receive an email with a DocuSign link
3. Review and sign the contract
4. Contractor signs
5. Contract becomes **ACTIVE** once both parties sign

## Milestones

### Understanding Milestones

Milestones break your project into manageable phases. Each milestone:
- Has a specific deliverable
- Has an associated payment amount
- Requires evidence submission
- Requires your approval before payment

### Milestone Status

- **PENDING**: Not yet started
- **SUBMITTED**: Contractor has submitted work for review
- **UNDER_REVIEW**: You are reviewing the submission
- **APPROVED**: Work approved, payment can be released
- **PAID**: Payment has been released
- **REJECTED**: Work needs revision

### Reviewing Submissions

1. **View Evidence**: Review photos, documents, and notes
2. **Check Requirements**: Compare against original requirements
3. **Add Comments**: Provide feedback or ask questions
4. **Approve or Reject**: Make your decision

### Approval Process

When approving:
- **Required**: Provide a reason for approval
- **Permit Check**: System verifies permit compliance
- **Payment Release**: Payment is automatically released (if escrow funded)

### Rejection Process

If rejecting:
- **Required**: Provide specific reasons
- **Comments**: Add detailed feedback
- **Resubmission**: Contractor can resubmit after making changes

## Payments

### Escrow System

All payments are protected through escrow:
- Funds are held securely until work is approved
- 10% holdback is retained until project completion
- Payments are released automatically upon milestone approval

### Making Payments

1. **Deposit to Escrow**: When contract is signed, deposit funds
2. **Automatic Release**: Payments release when milestones are approved
3. **Final Payment**: Released after closeout checklist completion

### Payment History

View all payment transactions:
- Milestone payments
- Final payment
- Refunds (if applicable)
- Transaction dates and amounts

### Escrow Balance

Monitor your escrow balance:
- Current balance
- Pending releases
- Holdback amount
- Available for release

## Disputes

### When to File a Dispute

File a dispute if:
- Work quality doesn't meet contract standards
- Contractor is not following the contract
- There are safety concerns
- Payment or timeline issues arise

### Filing a Dispute

1. Navigate to the relevant milestone or project
2. Click **"File Dispute"**
3. Provide:
   - **Reason**: Brief description of the issue
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation
   - **Priority**: Low, Normal, High, or Urgent
   - **Evidence**: Attach relevant documents or photos

### Dispute Process

1. **Filed**: Dispute is created and escrow is frozen
2. **Under Investigation**: System reviews the dispute
3. **Mediation**: If needed, mediation can be requested
4. **Resolution**: Dispute is resolved with an outcome

### Important Notes

- **Escrow Freeze**: Filing a dispute freezes escrow payments
- **Evidence**: Provide as much evidence as possible
- **Communication**: Use dispute comments to communicate
- **Resolution**: Disputes can be resolved in favor of owner, contractor, or partial settlement

## Closeout & Handoff

### Closeout Checklist

When all milestones are complete, you'll complete the closeout checklist:

1. **Final Inspection**: Ensure all inspections passed
2. **Lien Waiver Collection**: Collect lien waivers from contractor
3. **Punch List Resolution**: Complete any remaining items
4. **Warranty Documentation**: Collect all warranty documents
5. **Appliance Manuals**: Receive operation manuals
6. **As-Built Photos**: Upload final project photos
7. **Final Walkthrough**: Complete final walkthrough
8. **Document Archiving**: Archive all project documents
9. **Final Payment**: Release final holdback

### Completing Closeout

1. Navigate to project closeout page
2. Review all checklist items
3. Complete each item (upload documents, mark complete)
4. Click **"Complete Closeout"** when all required items are done
5. Final payment is automatically released

### Handoff Package

After closeout completion:

1. **Generate Package**: System creates a complete document package
2. **Download**: Download all project documents in one package
3. **Includes**:
   - All contracts
   - Permits and approvals
   - Inspection reports
   - Payment records
   - Warranty documentation
   - Project photos
   - Closeout documents

### Satisfaction Survey

After receiving your handoff package:
- Complete the satisfaction survey
- Rate your experience
- Provide feedback
- Help us improve the platform

### Common Issues

#### Can't Create Project

- **Check**: Verify permit compliance (if applicable)

- **Check**: Milestone must be approved

## FAQs

### General

**Q: Can I edit a project after creating it?**  
A: Yes, projects in DRAFT or READINESS status can be edited. Once in CONTRACTING or ACTIVE status, some fields are locked.

**Q: How do I cancel a project?**  
A: Contact support to cancel a project. Cancellation policies depend on project status and contract terms.

**Q: Can I use Kealee for multiple projects?**  
A: Yes, you can manage unlimited projects from your dashboard.

### Payments

**Q: How does escrow work?**  
A: Funds are deposited into a secure escrow account. Payments are released automatically when milestones are approved.

**Q: What is the holdback percentage?**  
A: Default is 10% of total contract value, held until project completion.

**Q: How long does payment take?**  
A: Payments are typically processed within 24-48 hours after milestone approval.

### Contracts

**Q: Can I modify a contract after signing?**  
A: Contract modifications require both parties to agree and sign an amendment.

**Q: What if a contractor doesn't sign?**  
A: Contracts expire after a set period. You can cancel and create a new contract with a different contractor.

### Milestones

**Q: Can I add milestones after contract signing?**  
A: Yes, but it requires a contract amendment signed by both parties.

**Q: What if I reject a milestone?**  
A: The contractor can make changes and resubmit. You'll review the updated submission.

### Disputes

**Q: How long does dispute resolution take?**  
A: Simple disputes: 3-5 business days. Complex disputes with mediation: 2-4 weeks.

**Q: Can I withdraw a dispute?**  
A: Yes, you can withdraw a dispute at any time. Escrow will be unfrozen.
