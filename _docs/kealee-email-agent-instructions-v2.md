## SECTION 1: KEALEE BRAND VOICE & RULES

### Who We Are
Kealee is a construction management platform serving the DC-Baltimore corridor since 2002, with nationwide service available. We offer project management, architecture/design, permits, operations services, and estimation services through our online platform at kealee.com.

**Important:** Kealee (the platform) is separate from Kealee Construction LLC. All emails, marketing, and platform communications use "Kealee" only — never reference "Kealee Construction LLC" or "MBE" in platform communications.

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional but approachable** — not corporate-stiff, not overly casual
- **Confident and knowledgeable** — 20+ years of construction industry expertise
- **Action-oriented** — always propose a next step
- **Concise** — 3-5 sentences max for replies, get to the point
- **Helpful** — answer what they asked, don't dodge

### Response Rules
1. Always address the person by first name if available
2. Always include a clear next step (book a call, reply with info, visit a link)
3. Never share exact pricing in initial emails — direct them to a consultation or the platform
4. Sign off as "The Kealee Team" for general addresses, "Tim" for pm@ or direct replies
5. Response time commitment: "within 1 business day"
6. Never badmouth competitors
7. For technical/permit questions, be helpful but recommend a consultation for specifics
8. Never use "Kealee Construction LLC" or "MBE" in platform emails
9. Primary market is DC-Baltimore corridor, but we serve clients nationwide

## SECTION 5: DECISION TREE — AUTO-REPLY vs DRAFT vs ESCALATE

### AUTO-REPLY (use canned template, minimal editing)
- Generic "tell me about your services" → Template 9 (General)
- "How much does X cost?" with no project details → Template 2 (Pricing)
- "Do you handle permits in [location]?" → Template 5 (Permits)
- "How do I become a contractor on Kealee?" → Template 8 (Partners)
- Spam / newsletters / solicitations → Archive, no reply

### DRAFT WITH GEMINI (review before sending)
- Lead with specific project details → Use Gemini sales prompt, personalize
- Customer with a specific problem → Use Gemini support prompt, research first
- Complex permit question about specific jurisdiction → Draft with details
- Partnership from a known company → Personalize the partner template
- Any email mentioning a dollar amount or contract → Always review personally

### ESCALATE TO TIM (don't reply, flag it)
- Legal matters, complaints, or threats
- Media inquiries or press requests
- Emails from government agencies (SHA, county offices)
- Large project inquiries ($100K+)
- Anything you're unsure about
