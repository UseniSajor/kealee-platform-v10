## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit the Kealee Platform signup page
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile information

### Step 2: Set Up Your Organization (Optional)

1. Navigate to **Organizations** in the main menu
2. Click **Create Organization**
3. Enter your organization name and details
4. Add team members if needed

### Step 3: Explore the Dashboard

- **Projects**: View all your active and completed projects
- **Contracts**: Manage your construction contracts
- **Payments**: Track payments and escrow balances
- **Settings**: Update your profile and preferences

## Creating Your First Project

### Project Creation Wizard

1. Click **+ New Project** from the dashboard
2. Fill in project details:
   - **Project Name**: e.g., "Kitchen Renovation"
   - **Category**: Select from Kitchen, Bathroom, Addition, etc.
   - **Description**: Brief description of your project
   - **Property**: Link to an existing property or create new
3. Click **Create Project**

### Project Status Flow

Your project will progress through these stages:

1. **DRAFT** → Initial project creation
2. **READINESS** → Complete readiness checklist
3. **CONTRACTING** → Create and sign contracts
4. **ACTIVE** → Construction in progress
5. **CLOSEOUT** → Final closeout checklist
6. **COMPLETED** → Project finished

## Readiness Checklist

Before starting construction, complete your readiness checklist.

### Accessing the Checklist

1. Navigate to your project
2. Click on the **Readiness Checklist** section
3. Review all required items

### Completing Items

1. Click on an incomplete item
2. Follow the instructions:
   - **Document Upload**: Upload required files
   - **Question/Answer**: Answer the question
   - **Confirmation**: Confirm completion
3. Add any notes or attachments
4. Click **Mark Complete**

### Bulk Completion

- Select multiple items using checkboxes
- Click **Complete Selected** to complete them all at once

### Moving to Next Stage

Once all **required** items are complete:
- The system will allow you to proceed to **CONTRACTING** status
- You'll see a confirmation message

## Contract Management

### Creating a Contract

1. From your project page, click **+ New Contract**
2. Fill in contract details:
   - **Contractor**: Select from marketplace or enter custom
   - **Amount**: Total contract value
   - **Payment Schedule**: Define milestone payments
   - **Timeline**: Project start and end dates
3. Review contract terms
4. Click **Create Contract**

### Contract Signing

1. Contract is sent via DocuSign to all parties
2. You'll receive an email notification
3. Click the link to review and sign
4. Once all parties sign, contract becomes **ACTIVE**

### Viewing Contracts

- View all contracts from the **Contracts** section
- Filter by status: Draft, Sent, Signed, Active, Completed
- Download signed contracts as PDF

## Milestone Tracking

### Understanding Milestones

Milestones break your project into manageable phases with defined deliverables and payments.

### Viewing Milestones

1. Navigate to your project
2. Click **View Milestone Dashboard**
3. See all milestones with their status:
   - **PENDING**: Not yet started
   - **SUBMITTED**: Contractor submitted for review
   - **UNDER_REVIEW**: You're reviewing
   - **APPROVED**: Approved, ready for payment
   - **PAID**: Payment released

### Reviewing Milestone Submissions

1. When a milestone is **SUBMITTED**, you'll receive a notification
2. Click on the milestone to review:
   - **Evidence**: Photos, documents, receipts
   - **Notes**: Contractor's completion notes
   - **Requirements**: Compare against original requirements
3. Review the evidence side-by-side with requirements
4. Add comments or request changes if needed

### Approving Milestones

1. Review all evidence and requirements
2. Check permit compliance status (if applicable)
3. Click **Approve Milestone**
4. Enter approval reason (required)
5. Confirm approval

**Note**: Milestones cannot be approved if:
- Permit compliance issues exist (unless overridden)
- Required evidence is missing
- Disputes are active

### Rejecting Milestones

1. If evidence doesn't meet requirements, click **Reject**
2. Enter rejection reason (required)
3. Add specific feedback for contractor
4. Milestone returns to contractor for resubmission

## Payment Processing

### Understanding Escrow

All project payments are held in escrow until milestones are approved, protecting both you and the contractor.

### Payment Release

1. Once a milestone is **APPROVED**, you can release payment
2. Navigate to the milestone detail page
3. Review payment summary:
   - Milestone amount
   - Holdback percentage (default 10%)
   - Net release amount
4. Optionally mark as "Final Payment" (skips holdback)
5. Add release notes
6. Click **Release Payment**

### Payment History

- View all payments from **Payments** section
- See transaction history:
  - Deposit dates
  - Release dates
  - Amounts
  - Status
- Download payment receipts

### Holdback

- 10% of each milestone is held back until project completion
- Final holdback is released during closeout
- Protects against incomplete work or warranty issues

## Dispute Resolution

### Filing a Dispute

If issues arise during construction:

1. Navigate to the relevant milestone
2. Click **File Dispute**
3. Fill in dispute details:
   - **Reason**: Brief reason for dispute
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation
   - **Priority**: Low, Normal, High, Urgent
4. Attach evidence (photos, documents)
5. Submit dispute

**Important**: Filing a dispute automatically freezes escrow payments until resolved.

### Dispute Resolution Process

1. **FILED**: Dispute submitted, escrow frozen
2. **UNDER_INVESTIGATION**: System/admin reviewing
3. **PENDING_MEDIATION**: Mediation requested
4. **RESOLVED**: Dispute resolved, escrow unfrozen

### Adding Comments

- Add comments to disputes for communication
- Internal comments (admin only) vs. external comments (visible to all parties)

## Project Closeout

### Closeout Checklist

When all milestones are complete:

1. Navigate to **Closeout Checklist** from project page
2. Complete all required items:
   - ✅ Final Inspection Completion
   - ✅ Lien Waiver Collection
   - ✅ Punch List Resolution
   - ✅ Warranty Documentation
   - ✅ Appliance & System Manuals
   - ✅ As-Built Photos
   - ✅ Final Walkthrough
   - ✅ Document Archiving
   - ✅ Final Payment Processing

### Punch List Management

1. Create punch list items for minor fixes
2. Assign to contractor
3. Track completion status
4. Verify fixes with photos

### Completing Closeout

1. Complete all required checklist items
2. Upload all required documents
3. Verify final payment release
4. Click **Complete Closeout**

This will:
- Release final holdback payment
- Mark project as **COMPLETED**
- Generate handoff package

## Handoff Package

### Generating Handoff Package

1. After closeout completion, navigate to **Handoff Package**
2. Click **Generate Handoff Package**
3. System automatically collects:
   - All contracts
   - Permits and approvals
   - Inspection reports
   - Payment records
   - Warranty documentation
   - Project photos
   - Closeout documents

### Downloading Package

1. Once generated, click **Download Package**
2. Package includes organized document bundles
3. Download individual documents or complete package

### Satisfaction Survey

1. Complete satisfaction survey after project completion
2. Rate your experience:
   - Overall rating (required)
   - Communication
   - Quality of work
   - Timeliness
   - Value for money
3. Provide feedback on what went well and improvements
4. Submit survey

## Getting Help

### Support Resources

- **Help Center**: Comprehensive documentation
- **Video Tutorials**: Step-by-step video guides
- **FAQ**: Frequently asked questions
- **Contact Support**: Email or chat support

### Common Issues

**Can't approve milestone?**
- Check permit compliance status
- Verify all required evidence is submitted
- Ensure no active disputes

**Payment not releasing?**
- Verify milestone is approved
- Check escrow balance
- Ensure no active disputes freezing escrow

**Contractor not responding?**
- Use dispute system to escalate
- Contact Kealee support for assistance

### Keyboard Shortcuts

- `Ctrl/Cmd + K`: Quick search
- `Ctrl/Cmd + /`: Show keyboard shortcuts
- `Esc`: Close modals/dialogs

## Best Practices

1. **Complete Readiness Early**: Finish readiness checklist before starting construction
2. **Review Milestones Promptly**: Approve or reject within 48 hours
3. **Communicate Clearly**: Use comments and notes for clarity
4. **Keep Documents Organized**: Upload all documents promptly
5. **Track Progress**: Regularly check milestone status
6. **Use Disputes Wisely**: Only file disputes for genuine issues
7. **Complete Closeout Thoroughly**: Ensure all items are complete before finalizing
