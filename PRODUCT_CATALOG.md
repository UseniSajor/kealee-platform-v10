## Platform-Wide Commission Structure

### How 3.5% Commission Works

1. **At Contract Ratification:**
   - Platform calculates 3.5% of total contract value
   - Creates PlatformFee record with status "PENDING"

2. **At First Milestone Payment:**
   - Fee status changes to "HOLD"
   - Amount held in escrow

3. **Upon Milestone Release:**
   - 3.5% deducted from contractor's payment
   - Remaining 96.5% released to contractor
   - Fee status changes to "COLLECTED"

### Example Calculation

| Contract Value | Platform Fee (3.5%) | Contractor Receives |
|---------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| $50,000 | $1,750 | $48,250 |
| $100,000 | $3,500 | $96,500 |
| $250,000 | $8,750 | $241,250 |
| $500,000 | $17,500 | $482,500 |
